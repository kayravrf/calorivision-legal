To request deletion of your KaloriVision account and all associated personal data, please send an email to calorivisionhelp@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request" In your email, please include: The email address you used to sign in to KaloriVision Your user ID (if available) After we receive your request, your account and associated data (such as profile information, meal history, and weight records) will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Some non-personal data may be retained for legal or accounting purposes if required by law.
